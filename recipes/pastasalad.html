Macaroni Salad

This is a lovely macaroni salad, completely vegan and cold, so perfect for hot summer evenings!

Ingredients

  • 200 grams macaroni
  • 250 grams vegan chicken
  • 2 bell peppers
  • 100 grams of leek
  • 1 tbsp vegetable stock powder
  • 2 tbsp vegan mayonnaise

Directions

  1. Boil Pasta accoding to directions
  2. Cut the vegan chicken in smaller pieces if desired, and prepare according to directions
  3. While the pasta and chicken are cooking, cut bell pepper and leek
  4. When the pasta is finished, drain and run cold water, so it cooles of
  5. Put together in bowl with bell pepper and leek and cooked chicken
  6. Add the stock powder and mayonnaise and mix well
  7. Enjoy!
